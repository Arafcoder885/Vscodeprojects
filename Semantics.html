Facts about dogs

Dogs are domesticated mammals, not natural wild animals. They were originally bred from wolves. They have been bred by humans for a long time, and were the first animals ever to be domesticated.

Dogs have been our companions for thousands of years, and they are known for their loyalty and intelligence.

There are hundreds of different breeds of dogs, each with unique characteristics and traits.
